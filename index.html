About me:

I studied a Master of Science in Machine Learning at University College London, advised by Prof. David Barber and Cristina Calnegru.My main areas of interest are generative modeling, language grounding and reinforcement learning. Before my Master, I completed my B.Sc. in Electro-Mechanical Engineering at "Tecnologico de Monterrey" (Mexico), and worked for 4 years in different energy industries and 1 year as an Entrepreneur.

At the moment, I work as a Machine Learning Engineer at Clyde & Co, an International Law Firm, focused on natural language processing tasks such as Named Entity Recognition and Relation Extraction, as well as Memory augmented neural networks, and Stochastic Processes.
